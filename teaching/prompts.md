# Teaching Prompts

A comprehensive collection of AI prompts for educators in scientific and academic contexts.

## Tips for Teaching-Related Prompts

1. **Specify Context**: Include student level, class size, subject area, and constraints.
2. **State Learning Goals**: What should students know or be able to do?
3. **Describe Students**: What background knowledge do they have? What challenges?
4. **Include Constraints**: Time limits, resources available, institutional context.
5. **Request Multiple Options**: Ask for several approaches to compare.
6. **Adapt to Your Style**: Use AI suggestions as starting points, adapt to your voice.
7. **Consider Accessibility**: Ask about making materials accessible to all students.
8. **Iterate Based on Results**: Refine activities based on what works in practice.
