# Research Discovery Flow

AI-assisted research exploration.

---

## Literature Review

(Prompt)
```
Based on <research topic>, generate a literature review report:
- use web search
- include >30 literature citations
- organize by themes/subtopics
- in markdown format
```

---

## Gap Identification

(Prompt)
```
What are the unresolved questions in <research area>?
- What methodological limitations exist?
- Where do researchers disagree?
```

---

## Powerful Prompts

```
Play devil's advocate: What are the strongest criticisms of <theory/method>?
```

```
What disciplines outside of <my field> study similar problems?
```

```
If I had unlimited resources, how would I study <question>? Now, what's the minimal viable version?
```

---

## Tips

1. **Verify citations**: AI may hallucinate paper titles
2. **Stay skeptical**: AI summarizes consensus; breakthroughs challenge it
